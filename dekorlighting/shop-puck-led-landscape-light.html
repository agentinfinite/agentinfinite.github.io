Puck LED Landscape Light – Outdoor Uplighting Made Easy

Create Dramatic Effects. While many know puck lights as part of a cabinet lighting project, they can also create a warm, welcoming ambiance outside. This low profile LED landscape light does one thing and does it well: uplighting. It can be positioned on any yard surface for subtle accent lighting, no digging required. Many use this product for tree uplighting, to call out certain garden features or shrubs, or to illuminate a 90° corner. The light can also be nestled in pebbles or stones for a very attractive “flush-mount, recessed” look. You can choose between 6 different color options to complement your landscape.

Benefits of DEKOR Puck Lights:

  • Waterproof
  • Warm white light
  • Dimmable LEDs (CREE)
  • Drop and Go Installation
  • Robust, Solid Construction
  • Energy efficient
  • Long lasting and Durable, backed by our 5 year / 30 year warranty
  • Made in USA

DEKOR® Lighting features Cree LEDs at a precise 3200 Kelvin. This color temperature strikes the perfect balance of warm and cool, creating an elegant look that makes any home shine.

DEKOR® is proud to announce that this product is now part of our official product partnership with Cree Venture LED®, a joint venture between Cree, Inc. and San’an Optoelectronics Co., Ltd.
