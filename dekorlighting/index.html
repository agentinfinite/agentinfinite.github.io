DEKOR Lighting Home Tour

Join our CEO & founder, Duane McCall, as he shows you how he has incorporated DEKOR LED lighting into his home’s indoor and outdoor spaces.

You’ll get to learn about the full spectrum of our products from landscape and deck lights to under cabinet light kits in the kitchen.See what an impact our small fixtures make and how easy it is to install them anywhere.

No matter the application, DEKOR is sure to have a lighting solution that is perfect for your home or business.

Welcome to DEKOR®, a leading manufacturer, designer and innovator of LED lighting products.

Here at DEKOR®, we create trendsetting LED outdoor lights to complement your deck, landscape, hardscape and more, as well as indoor lighting options. We design and build the best products possible in order to deliver the best quality out there. We design, manufacture, and test our world-class quality LED lighting right here in the USA, so we can manage every aspect of our lights, from conception to manufacturing.

We build lighting for life— Our products are built to last and enhance your lifestyle. Customers love the quality and warmth of our lights, and the way our innovative LED lights transform their homes and outdoor living spaces. When you choose DEKOR®, you can gain peace of mind with our LED indoor and outdoor lighting products. You can count on:

  • Ease of installation – DIYers & pros alike can install DEKOR products, thanks to our Plug-N-Play technology.
  • Durability – All of our outdoor lights are robust, waterproof and built to last through extreme weather & wear
  • Reliability – Our lights exceed industry standards for reliability and performance
  • Superior quality – Our lights use best-in-class CREE LEDs. Many of our low voltage products are available with warm white light bulbs or multiple colors.
  • Energy savings – You’ll save money with our LEDs, which are much more energy efficient than incandescent bulbs.
  • Safety certifications – Our products meet ETL, CE and RCM standards
  • Outstanding warranties – We back the quality of our products with leading warranties
  • Support – Our care team works right alongside our builders at our factory in Denver, CO.
  • Mobile app – The DEKOR WiFi Remote App brings home automation and smart control to DEKOR Lighting projects: On/Off, Brightness and Scheduling.
  • Dimmable options – Our dimmers and timers give you complete control of your ambiance.

If you’re looking for the best outdoor LED lighting solutions, DEKOR has you covered. From small discreet Dek Dots to high output landscape lighting, you’re sure to find the right product to fit your needs. Shop our entire collection today.
